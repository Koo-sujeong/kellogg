DIRECTIONS
  1. Use 1 3/4 - 2-inch cookie cutter(s) coated with cooking spray to cut 8 desired shapes from Pop-Tarts®. Set aside.
  2. In a medium bowl, stir together strawberries and sugar.
  3. Place 1 Pop-Tart® shape in each of 4 dessert dishes. Spoon strawberries on top. Add dollop of whipped topping to each dish. Stand remaining Pop-Tart® shapes in whipped topping. Serve immediately.
DIRECTIONS
  1. Cut one 3-inch triangle from the corner of each Pop-Tart®. Coarsely crumble remaining Pop-Tart® pieces. Set aside.
  2. In blender container combine ice cream, milk and vanilla. Cover and blend just until smooth. Add Pop-Tart® crumbs (reserve triangle pieces for garnish). Blend just until combined.
  3. Pour ice cream mixture into two 16-ounce serving glasses. Garnish each with a Pop-Tart® triangle. Serve immediately.
DIRECTIONS
  1. Use 2 1 / 2- to 2 3/4-inch heart-shaped cookie cutter coated with cooking spray to cut out a heart from each Pop-Tart®.
  2. Use white frosting to write the words "Be Mine" or another message on each.
