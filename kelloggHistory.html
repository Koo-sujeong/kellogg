HISTORY

From feeding the US Army to going to the moon to making
everyday moments G-r-r-r-eat!

HISTORY

  • 1894 - 1950

  • 1952 - 1999

  • 2000 - 2015

  • 2016 - 2020

  1. 1894

    THE CREATION W.K. Kellogg changed breakfast forever by creating Corn Flakes.

  2. 1906

    THE FOUNDING W.K. Kellogg opened the “Battle Creek Toasted Corn Flake Company”

  3. 1914

    CORN FLAKES TO CANADA Kellogg’s® Corn Flakes® was introduced in Canada.

  4. 1915

    BRAN FLAKES Kellogg introduced Bran Flakes, the first high-fiber cereal

  5. 1923

    KELLOGG'S 1ST DIETICIAN Kellogg Company hired a dietitian, Mary Barber, to help create a proper diet.

  6. 1928

    RICE KRISPIES DEBUT Kellogg's Rice Krispies were released

  7. 1930s

    WK KELLOGG FOUNDATION FORMED The W.K. Kellogg Foundation was founded, to help children realize their potential

  8. 1940s

    SUPPORTING US TROOPS Kellogg’s employees produced K-rations for the U.S. armed forces during WWII

  9. 1950

    CORN POPS RELEASED Corn Pops released

  1. 1952

    MEET TONY Tony the Tiger was introduced along with Sugar Frosted Flakes

  2. 1953

    FIRST HONEY SMACKS The present day Honey Smacks was introduced as Sugar Smacks

  3. 1955

    SPECIAL K DEBUT Special K® was released and became the first cereal with seven vitamins and iron

  4. 1958

    MEET CORNELIUS Introduction of Cornelius.

  5. 1963

    FIRST FROOT LOOPS Froot Loops introduced

  6. 1970

    FIRST FROSTED MINI WHEATS Kellogg's® Frosted Mini-Wheats® cereal debuted nationally.

  7. 1991

    NEW NUTRI-GRAIN Nutri-Grain® Bars released in 1991.

  8. 1997

    WKKI FOUNDING The W.K. Kellogg Institute for Food and Nutrition Research was opened.

  9. 1999

    MEET MORNING STAR FARMS Kellogg acquired Morning Star Farms soy-based vegetarian foods

  1. 2000

    HELL KASHI Kellogg acquired Kashi Cereal

  2. 2001

    KEEBLER ACQUISITION Kellogg purchases Keebler Company

  3. 2006

    100 YEARS Kellogg celebrated its 100th anniversary and created 100-calorie snack.

  4. 2012

    KRAVE HITS SHELVES Krave cereal was introduced & Kellogg acquired Pringles.

  5. 2015

    TOLARAM AND BISCO MISR Formed Tolaram Africa Joint Venture. Acquired stake in Bisco Misr and Mass Food Group.

  1. 2016

    PARATI Acquired Parati, leading Brazilian biscuit company

  2. 2017

    RXBAR Acquired RXBAR, fastest growing US nutrition bar brand

  3. 2018

    HAPPY INSIDEA Debuts HI! Happy Inside™ Cereal That Supports Digestive Wellness

  4. 2019

    MISSION TIGER Launch of Mission Tiger across the USA

  5. 2020

    WK KELLOGG BY KIDS CEREAL W.K. Kellogg by Kids cereal in Europe was introduced
