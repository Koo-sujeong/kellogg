ABOUT THIS ITEM

DETAILS With three flavors to choose from, our cheesy baked crackers are individually packed for convenience and make a delicious anytime snack! Cheez-It® Variety features Cheez-It® Cheddar Jack, Cheez-It® White Cheddar, and the iconic Cheez-It® Original. They're perfect for school lunches, after school snacks, on-the-go bites, or picnics in the park.
